Wii Hacking Tutorial

For this guide, we will be using the LetterBomb method.

Note: There are other ways to homebrew your Wii; this guide just doesn't cover them.

Turn on your Wii and go to settings. Once there, go to Internet then Console Information and grab your MAC address. Go to please.hackmii.com. It should look something like this:

Put your Wii's MAC address into the bubble and make sure your region is set correctly and the HackMi installer is bundled. Cut either wire and wait for the file to download. Plug your SD card into your computer and extract the files to the root of your SD card. Your SD card should look something like this:

Take out your sd card and enter it into your Nintendo Wii, Then open your Wii Message Board and open the letter bomb icon. (If you cant see it look a day behind or a day ahead).

Press 1 on your Wiimote after waiting 30 seconds on the scam warning screen. Select continue, then install the homebrew channel. After that go back and select BootMii and install it as an IOS (If you can install BootMii as boot2 do that aswell). Press exit and you will enter the Homebrew Channel! Once there press the home button and launch BootMii press the reset button until the gear icon is selected then press power. Press the power button again and wait for the NAND to backup to the SD card. Exit BootMii.
