Wiimmfi Install guide

Note: Priiloader does a lot more than just patch your games for Wiimmfi it also adds extra brick protection and much more!

Before we can start playing games online with Wiimmfi we must install Priiloader to patch our games for us. First download the Priiloader files here and here. Extract the contents on to the root of the SD card and open it on the HBC (Homebrew Channel) Press the plus button on your Wiimote then press A to go back to HBC delete the Priiloader file then open up the second thing "LoadPriiloader" go to system menu hacks and press A. Select Block Disc Updates, Block Online Updates, Region Free Everything, and Wiimmfi patch v4. Save your settings then press B to exit.



Great now all your disc games will be pactched to work with Wiimmfi press here to head back to the options page!
