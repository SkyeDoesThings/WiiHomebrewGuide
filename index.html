Wii Homebrew Guide

About Homebrew

Homebrew applications are games or apps that have not been officially licensed by Nintendo themselves. These apps can't be loaded onto a console with modifications made to the system NAND (the memory on the console). Thankfully, that is a very easy task to achieve by following this guide!

Requirements

Homebrewing your Wii is really easy, and you don't need many things!

  • Fat 32 formatted SD card
  • A Wii on the latest version 4.3
  • Something to read your SD card

What Can You Do with Homebrew?

Great questions! Just to list a few things:

  • Back up your disc games and load them on something like USB Loader GX
  • Play homebrew Wii games
  • Bring back shut down services with things like WiiLink, Wiimmfi, and Riiconnect24
